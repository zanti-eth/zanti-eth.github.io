# <a id="intro"></a>🎮 What is CryptoRoyale?

[CryptoRoyale.one](https://cryptoroyale.one) is a **free-to-play** browser-based battle royale crypto game. Players compete using colored circular avatars and fight until only one survives.

🧠 **Skill-based:** Movement, timing, and power-up strategy determine the winner — **not money**.

🏅 **Earn ROY tokens** by placing in the top 3 of each game.

# <a id="how-to-play"></a>🕹️ How to Play

1. Visit [https://cryptoroyale.one](https://cryptoroyale.one)
2. Choose **Quick Play** or **Custom Rooms**
3. Control your player with **mouse or touch**:
   - Move with cursor
   - Dodge lasers & absorb power-ups
4. Survive the shrinking map and be the last one standing!

🔵 Blue = Normal  
🟡 Yellow = Speed  
🔴 Red = Attack  

Power-ups change your color and boost abilities!

# <a id="roy"></a>💰 What is ROY?

**ROY** is the native token of CryptoRoyale, rewarded for gameplay performance.

| Feature | Details |
|--------|---------|
| 🎯 Use Case | Rewards, staking, governance |
| 🌐 Chains | Avalanche, Polygon, Harmony ONE |
| 🔄 Exchange | Listed on [CoinGecko](https://www.coingecko.com/en/coins/cryptoroyale) & [DEXs](https://cryptoroyale.one/roy) |
| 🤑 Distribution | Earned by playing or staking ROY/ONE liquidity |

> 🚀 ROY has zero supply inflation — it's redistributed from prize pools and buybacks.

# <a id="wallet"></a>🔐 Connecting a Wallet

To **claim or withdraw ROY**, connect your crypto wallet:

✅ Supported wallets:
- MetaMask
- Rabby Wallet
- Trust Wallet (via WalletConnect)

✅ Supported networks:
- **Avalanche**
- **Polygon**
- **Harmony ONE**

Switch your wallet to one of the networks before claiming.

> 🛠️ **No wallet required to play** — but needed to claim ROY or join staking pools.

# <a id="tips"></a>🧠 Tips & Strategy

- **Stay near the center** of the shrinking arena
- Use **speed (yellow)** to dodge and position better
- Save **red power-ups** for the final fight
- Bait enemies into lasers or the shrinking wall
- Practice in custom lobbies or with bots

> 👑 The more you win, the higher your **XP, rewards & leaderboard ranking**.

# <a id="faq"></a>❓ FAQ

### Is CryptoRoyale free?

> Yes! 100% free-to-play. You only need a wallet to **claim ROY**.

### Where can I sell my ROY tokens?

> Use [ViperSwap](https://viperswap.one), [Trader Joe](https://traderjoexyz.com), or [QuickSwap](https://quickswap.exchange), depending on your network.

### Can I play on mobile?

> Yes — the game works in most mobile browsers (Chrome, Safari). A gamepad is optional.
