# 😨 FUD

**FUD** stands for **Fear, Uncertainty, and Doubt** — a term used when someone spreads negative or misleading information to influence markets.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"The media is spreading FUD again about crypto regulation."*

### 🙅‍♂️ Counter It
- Learn the facts, don't fall for manipulation.
