# 📈📉 Pump and Dump

A **Pump and Dump** is a scheme where a token’s price is artificially pumped (hyped) and then quickly sold off by insiders.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"This token is a classic pump and dump."*

### 🚨 Warning
- Always research before investing in hyped coins.
