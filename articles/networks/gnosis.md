# 🌐 Gnosis Chain: The Scalable and Secure Ethereum Sidechain (formerly xDai)

The **Gnosis Chain**, formerly known as **xDai**, is a community-owned Ethereum sidechain designed for **low-cost, high-speed transactions**. Built for real-world adoption, Gnosis Chain bridges the gap between scalability and decentralization.

If you’re looking to dive into a blockchain that balances affordability, security, and decentralization — this is the chain you should be paying attention to.

---

## 🧠 What is Gnosis Chain?

Gnosis Chain is a **fully EVM-compatible**, **proof-of-stake** blockchain that operates as an Ethereum sidechain. It was launched in 2018 under the name **xDai Chain**, focusing on enabling everyday transactions using a stablecoin-based gas token (xDai).

In late 2021, the chain was **merged into the Gnosis ecosystem** — an OG Ethereum project known for its prediction markets, Gnosis Safe, and commitment to decentralization.

> ✅ **Key features**:
> - Super low transaction fees (fractions of a cent)
> - 5-second block times
> - Decentralized validator set (over 200 validators)
> - Native bridges to Ethereum and other chains
> - EVM compatibility — deploy Ethereum smart contracts with no changes

---

## 🔁 From xDai to Gnosis: Why the Rebrand?

The transition from **xDai** to **Gnosis Chain** reflected a deeper integration into the Gnosis ecosystem. It was more than a name change — it marked a strategic move to:

- Strengthen security via Gnosis’ infrastructure
- Expand validator decentralization
- Align with GnosisDAO’s community governance

The core mission remains: **an Ethereum-aligned, community-first, scalable blockchain for real-world use**.

---

## ⚙️ How Gnosis Chain Works

Gnosis Chain uses a **dual-token model**:

1. **GNO (Gnosis Token)** – Used for staking and governance
2. **xDAI** – A stablecoin used for gas fees and transactions

### 🔐 Proof-of-Stake (via Gnosis Beacon Chain)
- GNO holders can **stake GNO** to run validators or delegate
- Over 200 validators ensure decentralized block production
- Staking is permissionless and supports community validators

### 🔗 Bridging with Ethereum
- Gnosis Bridge allows you to move assets between **Ethereum ↔ Gnosis Chain**
- Native support for bridging ETH, DAI, USDC, GNO, and many more

### 🤝 3. **Strong Community & Governance**
- Governed by **GnosisDAO**
- Active development via **Gnosis Builders** and contributors

### 🔐 4. **Battle-Tested Tools**
- Native home of **Gnosis Safe** — the most trusted multisig in Web3
- Supports tools like Dune Analytics, The Graph, Chainlink, Snapshot

## 🏛️ Staking and Validators

You can stake your GNO tokens and earn rewards while helping secure the chain.

- **Minimum to run a validator**: 1 GNO
- Delegation available via third-party tools like [Dappnode](https://dappnode.io) or [Staking Platforms](https://docs.gnosischain.com/validators/staking-overview)

> Gnosis Chain is among the **most decentralized EVM chains**, with a rapidly growing validator set.
