# 🚫 NGMI

**NGMI** stands for "Not Gonna Make It" — used when someone is making bad decisions or losing hope.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"Sold the bottom again. NGMI."*

### 😢 Tone
- Sometimes humorous, sometimes serious.
