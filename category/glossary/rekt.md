# 💥 REKT

**REKT** is a dramatic way of saying someone got "wrecked" — typically due to a bad trade or market crash.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"I went all in on $XYZ... got REKT."*

### 💸 Meaning
- Severe losses or failures in crypto, DeFi, or NFT trades.
