# 🎒 Bagholder

A **Bagholder** is someone holding crypto tokens that have dropped significantly in value — often because they refused to sell during a downturn.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"I’m still holding $XYZ from 2021... total bagholder now."*

### 💼 Psychology
- Denial, hope, or pure conviction.
