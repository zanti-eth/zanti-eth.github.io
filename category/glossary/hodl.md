# 🛑 HODL

**HODL** originally came from a drunken typo on a Bitcoin forum in 2013, where someone wrote "I AM HODLING" instead of "holding."

It has since become a meme and a mindset in the crypto world:  
> Hold your coins through all market ups and downs.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"Bitcoin is crashing!"*  
  → _"Relax, I'm HODLing."_

### 🙌 Philosophy
- HODL represents belief in long-term value, ignoring short-term volatility.
