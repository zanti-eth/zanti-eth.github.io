# 🧼 Rugpull

A **Rugpull** is when the creators of a crypto project vanish after collecting investor funds — leaving everyone holding worthless tokens.

### 🔥 Usage
- *"They deleted the Discord and website. Total rugpull."*

### ⚠️ Stay Safe
- Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research).
